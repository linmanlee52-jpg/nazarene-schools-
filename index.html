Who We Are

Welcome to Nazarene Progress School, a beacon of academic excellence in Mabogini, Moshi. We are dedicated to providing a transformative educational experience that prepares students for a global future while remaining deeply rooted in our Tanzanian heritage.

Our Journey

Nazarene Schools began its journey in 2004 with a vision to transform the educational landscape in Tanzania. Over the years, we have evolved into a modern institution that balances tradition with innovation.

Our Educational Approach

We take pride in offering a comprehensive curriculum. Our academic program blends the National Curriculum with international standards, providing our students with a competitive edge.

Life at Nazarene

To showcase the holistic care at Nazarene Progress School, this section highlights our commitment to ensuring children are safe, well-fed, and spiritually nurtured.

🚌 Transport & Logistics

We understand the importance of a safe and punctual commute. Our dedicated school bus service ensures that distance is never a barrier to quality education.

  • Broad Coverage: Our buses operate across Moshi Town and surrounding areas, including Mtakuja, Msere, and all central town locations.
  • Safety First: Experienced drivers and staff ensure children are picked up and dropped off safely and on time every day.

🏠 Boarding & Accommodation

Our boarding facility is designed to be a "home away from home." We provide a nurturing environment where students grow into well-rounded individuals.

  • High-Quality Dormitories: Clean, modern, and secure living spaces.
  • Professional Pastoral Care: Skilled Patrons and Matrons dedicated to raising your child spiritually, mentally, and academically.

🍽️ Nutrition & Dining

A healthy body leads to a healthy mind. We take pride in our kitchen, which serves fresh, delicious, and balanced meals.

  • Balanced Diet Pyramid: Menu strictly designed around proper nutrition.
  • Quality Ingredients: Healthy food fueling students for learning and activities.
